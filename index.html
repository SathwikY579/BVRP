About Me

A Leader Committed to Service

Inspired by the vision of Pawan Kalyan Garu, I joined Janasena Party in 2014 with a commitment to serve my community and uphold the values of justice, integrity, and social welfare. My journey from a grassroots volunteer to an MPTC representative reflects my dedication to meaningful political service.

Education: B.Com Graduate
Community: Kapu (OC)
Location: Virawada, Pithapuram
Constituency: Kakinada District

My political journey has been guided by the principle that true leadership means serving those who need it most. From animal welfare to community development, every initiative I undertake is rooted in the Janasena ideology of selfless service.

My Political Journey

2014

Joined Janasena Party

Inspired by Pawan Kalyan Garu's vision for social justice and political reform, I became an active member of Janasena Party, beginning my journey in grassroots politics.

2015

Community Organizer

Started organizing community welfare programs and became a voice for local issues in Virawada, establishing strong connections with constituents.

2018

Animal Welfare Initiative

Launched major cow rescue operations as Gau Rakshak, successfully rescuing over 1000 cows and establishing animal welfare as a key priority.

2019

MPTC Representative

Elected as MPTC, representing the people of Virawada and working on infrastructure development, healthcare access, and educational initiatives.

2020

Pandemic Relief Work

Led COVID-19 relief efforts, organizing food distribution, medical assistance, and support for affected families during the challenging pandemic period.

2021

Welfare Trust Leadership

Appointed as Vice President of a welfare trust, expanding social service initiatives and implementing sustainable community development programs.

2024

Policy Advocacy

Successfully banned weekly cow sales in Pithapuram, demonstrating commitment to animal rights and ethical governance at the policy level.

2025

Continued Service

Expanding outreach programs and strengthening Janasena's presence in the region while maintaining focus on grassroots development and social justice.

My Mission Beyond Politics

🐄

Gau Rakshak Initiative

Successfully rescued over 2,500+ cows from dangerous situations, providing them with safe shelter and medical care. This initiative reflects our commitment to protecting innocent animals and upholding dharmic values.

❌

Policy Reform

Led the successful campaign to ban weekly cow sales in Pithapuram, creating a precedent for animal rights protection and demonstrating effective policy advocacy at the grassroots level.

👥

Welfare Trust Leadership

Serving as Vice President of a prominent welfare trust, overseeing multiple community development projects and ensuring transparent, effective delivery of social services.

🍛

Community Infrastructure

Organized hospital food services, installed community benches, erected inspirational statues, and established chalivendrams to support the spiritual and practical needs of our community.
